---
title: "Browser Compatibility"
description: "Supported browsers and troubleshooting for 3DMapFi."
---

3DMapFi is designed to work across all modern browsers.

## Supported Browsers

- Chrome (latest)
- Firefox (latest)
- Edge (latest)
- Safari (latest)

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, ensure your browser is up to date and supports WebGL 2.0.

For more help, contact [support](mailto:3dmapfi@gmail.com).
